********
Features
********

- Integrated 1-Wire to I²C master bridge DS28E17 (Maxim)
- Operating voltage range: 2.5 V to 3.6 V
- Operating temperature range: -20 to 70 °C
- Mechanical dimensions: 33 x 55 mm
